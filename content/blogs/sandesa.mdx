## What is Sandesa?

Sandesa is an AI-powered newspaper that fetches articles from various RSS feeds, extracts their contents and generates concise summaries. Instead of overwhelming content with dozens of headlines, it presents only the most important stories.

The goal is simple:\
Read less. Learn more.

- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Supabase

## The Biggest Challenge

The biggest challenge was token and RPD. Even the worst model of gemini had 20 RPD for free-tier. We minimized tokens by cleaning input before we send. We even switched to Groq since it had more RPD (but less tokens).
